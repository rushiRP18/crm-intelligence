## 2. Plan Details

### 2.1 Free Plan

- **Price:** $0/month forever
- **Seats:** 1 user only (no team access)
- **API Rate Limit:** 100 requests per minute
- **Storage:** 500 MB
- **Features:** Core CRM functionality, basic lead tracking, community support forums
- **Limitations:** No API access to premium endpoints, no webhook support, no custom reports
- **Upgrade Path:** Upgrade to Standard at any time; pro-rata billing applies from day of upgrade

### 2.2 Standard Plan

- **Price:** $99/month (billed monthly) or $950/year (billed annually — saves ~$238/year)
- **Seats:** Up to 10 seats; additional seats available at $12/seat/month
- **API Rate Limit:** 1,000 requests per minute
- **Storage:** 50 GB
- **Features:**
  - All Free features
  - Full API access (v2 endpoints)
  - Webhook support
  - Email support with 48-hour response SLA
  - Standard reports and dashboards
  - CRM pipeline management
  - 1,000 AI-assisted interactions/month
- **Overage:** API requests exceeding rate limits are queued (not dropped) up to 2× the limit, then rate-limited with HTTP 429

### 2.3 Pro Plan

- **Price:** $299/month (billed monthly) or $2,870/year (billed annually — saves ~$718/year)
- **Seats:** Up to 25 seats; additional seats available at $15/seat/month
- **API Rate Limit:** 5,000 requests per minute
- **Storage:** 500 GB
- **Features:**
  - All Standard features
  - Priority email and live chat support (4-hour response SLA)
  - Advanced analytics and custom reports
  - Custom integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zapier, etc.)
  - Advanced AI features: sentiment analysis, churn prediction, opportunity scoring
  - 10,000 AI-assisted interactions/month
  - Dedicated onboarding session (2 hours)
  - Access to beta features

### 2.4 Enterprise Plan

- **Price:** Custom pricing — contact sales@company.com or call 1-800-CRM-INTEL
- **Seats:** Unlimited
- **API Rate Limit:** 10,000+ requests per minute (configurable per agreement)
- **Storage:** Unlimited (data warehousing options available)
- **Features:**
  - All Pro features
  - Dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM)
  - Custom SLA agreements (up to 99.99% uptime)
  - White-label deployment option
  - HIPAA-compliant deployment (with BAA)
  - GDPR Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

  - SSO/SAML integration

  - Quarterly business reviews (QBR)
  - 24/7 phone and emergency support
  - Reseller/white-label program eligibility

## 3. Billing Policies

### 3.1 Pro-Rata Billing (Mid-Cycle Upgrades)

When a customer upgrades their plan mid-billing cycle, the following pro-rata calculation applies:

**Formula:**
```
Upgrade Charge = (New Plan Monthly Price - Current Plan Monthly Price)
                 × (Remaining Days in Cycle / Total Days in Cycle)
```

**Example:**
- Customer on Standard ($99/month) upgrades to Pro ($299/month) on Day 15 of a 30-day cycle
- Remaining days: 15
- Pro-rata charge: ($299 − $99) × (15/30) = $200 × 0.5 = **$100.00**
- Full Pro plan billing begins at the next cycle start

**Notes:**
- Pro-rata charges are applied immediately at the time of upgrade
- Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle (no refund for unused days)
- Seat additions within a plan also follow pro-rata billing for the current cycle

### 3.2 Annual Billing

- Annual plans are billed in full at the start of the subscription year
- **Discount:** 20% off the equivalent monthly pricing
- Annual plans are non-refundable after the 14-day refund window (see Refund Policy)
- Seat additions to annual plans: billed monthly at the add-on seat rate for the remainder of the annual term
- Renewal: automatic renewal 30 days before expiration; cancellation notice required at least 14 days before renewal date

### 3.3 Payment Methods

- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- ACH bank transfer (Enterprise only)
- Wire transfer (Enterprise only, minimum $10,000/year commitment)
- Purchase orders (Enterprise only)

### 3.4 Taxes

- Applicable sales tax, VAT, or GST may be added based on customer location
- US customers: sales tax added where required by law
- EU customers: VAT applied at the applicable rate for the customer's country
- Tax-exempt organizations must provide valid exemption certificate before invoicing

## 4. Discount Programs

### 4.1 Non-Profit Discount

**Eligibility:** Registered 501(c)(3) organizations (US) or equivalent non-profit status in other countries.

- **Discount:** 30% off Standard and Pro plans (monthly or annual)
- **Free plan:** Not eligible (already free)
- **Enterprise plan:** Non-profit pricing negotiated individually; contact sales

**How to Apply:**
1. Submit a request to billing@company.com with subject line: "Non-Profit Discount Request"
2. Attach a copy of your IRS 501(c)(3) determination letter (or equivalent documentation)
3. Provide your organization's legal name and EIN (Employer Identification Number)
4. Allow 5–7 business days for verification

**Ongoing Requirements:**
- Non-profit status must be re-verified annually
- Discount is revoked if the organization loses non-profit status
- Non-profit pricing cannot be combined with other discounts

**Effective Pricing with Non-Profit Discount:**

| Plan       | Standard Price | Non-Profit Price (30% off) |
|------------|---------------|---------------------------|
| Standard   | $99/month      | $69.30/month              |
| Standard Annual | $950/year | $665/year               |
| Pro        | $299/month     | $209.30/month             |
| Pro Annual | $2,870/year   | $2,009/year               |

### 4.2 Academic / Educational Pricing

**Eligibility:** Accredited colleges, universities, K-12 schools, and educational research institutions.

- **Discount:** 50% off Standard plan (monthly or annual)
- **Pro plan:** 25% off for academic institutions with 10+ seats
- **Enterprise:** Academic pricing negotiated individually

**How to Apply:**
1. Email billing@company.com with subject line: "Academic Pricing Request"
2. Provide: institution name, .edu email address, and proof of accreditation
3. Verification within 3–5 business days

**Effective Pricing with Academic Discount:**

| Plan             | Standard Price  | Academic Price (50% off) |
|------------------|-----------------|--------------------------|
| Standard         | $99/month        | $49.50/month             |
| Standard Annual  | $950/year        | $475/year                |

**Note:** Academic pricing is not combinable with non-profit discounts.

### 4.3 Volume Discounts (50+ Seats)

For organizations requiring more than 50 seats across any plan, volume discounts apply:

| Seat Range  | Discount on Per-Seat Add-On Price |
|-------------|-----------------------------------|
| 51–100 seats | 10% off add-on seat price        |
| 101–250 seats | 20% off add-on seat price       |
| 251–500 seats | 30% off add-on seat price       |
| 500+ seats  | Custom pricing — contact sales    |

Volume discounts are applied to the per-seat add-on cost, not the base plan price.

**Example:** Pro plan (25 seats included) + 75 additional seats:
- Add-on seat price: $15/seat/month
- Volume discount (51–100 range): 10% off
- Discounted add-on price: $13.50/seat/month
- Monthly add-on cost for 75 seats: $1,012.50

### 4.4 Startup Program

**Eligibility:** Companies less than 3 years old with fewer than 20 employees and less than $1M in annual revenue.

- **Benefit:** 6 months of Pro plan at 50% off, then standard pricing
- **Application:** startup@company.com with pitch deck or LinkedIn company page

### 4.5 Partner and Referral Program

- Referring customers who sign Standard or Pro plans earn a 10% credit on the referred customer's first 3 months
- Credits applied to referrer's next invoice
- Referral links available in account dashboard

## 5. White-Label / Reseller Program

The white-label and reseller program is available exclusively to Enterprise plan customers. This program enables partners to rebrand and resell the CRM Intelligence Platform under their own brand.

**To qualify and get details:**
- Must be on the Enterprise plan
- Contact sales@company.com or your dedicated Account Executive
- Separate Reseller Agreement required

**Key Features of the Reseller Program:**
- Custom branding (logo, color scheme, domain)
- Reseller margin: negotiated based on volume commitments
- Dedicated partner portal
- Co-marketing opportunities
- Partner onboarding and certification program

## 6. Plan Changes and Cancellation

### 6.1 Upgrading

- Effective immediately upon confirmation
- Pro-rata charge applied (see Section 3.1)

### 6.2 Downgrading

- Effective at the end of the current billing cycle
- No refunds for unused days on the higher-tier plan
- Data/features from higher tier remain accessible until cycle end

### 6.3 Cancellation

- Monthly plans: cancel anytime, access retained until end of current billing period
- Annual plans: cancel before renewal date; no refunds for remaining annual term after 14-day window
- See Refund Policy for full details on refund eligibility

---

## 7. Price Change Notifications

- CRM Intelligence Platform reserves the right to change pricing with **30 days advance notice** via email and in-product notification
- Existing subscribers are grandfathered at their current rate until their next renewal after the price change effective date
- Annual plan holders are not subject to price changes until their annual renewal
